Biography

Shiyi Lan is a Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Scienceat University of Maryland, College Park(UMD), supervised by
Prof. Larry S. Davis. He earned his Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science from Fudan University. Currently, he is research intern at NVIDIA AI Research supervised by Dr. Zhiding Yu and Prof. Animashree Anandkumar . His research interests lie in the field of
Scene Understanding, 2D and 3D Object Detection, Instance Segmentation, Correspondence Learning.
